Community Guidelines

Last Updated: [Update Date]

CG Chartbusters is a respectful and community-driven platform dedicated to Chhattisgarhi entertainment.

To maintain a positive environment, users must follow these rules:


1. Respectful Conduct

No abusive, hateful, or discriminatory language.

No harassment of artists or other users.


2. Honest Reviews

Reviews must reflect genuine opinions.

Fake ratings, paid manipulation, or spam activity is prohibited.


3. No Illegal Content

Do not post pirated links.

Do not promote illegal downloads.

Do not upload copyrighted material without permission.


4. No Defamation

Avoid false allegations against artists, creators, or businesses.

You are legally responsible for your statements.


5. No Impersonation

Do not pretend to be an artist, public figure, or official representative.


6. Enforcement

Violation of these guidelines may result in:

Content removal

Account suspension

Permanent ban

Legal action (if required)

CG Chartbusters reserves the right to moderate content at its discretion.
